HOME HEALTH

Ennoble Care Home Health Care assists our clients in a one-on-one home setting. Our nurses and certified home health aides are thoroughly screened, trained, and supervised by an internal Director of Nursing and provide care with a strong foundation of Care as an Honor.

Serving Patients in: NY, NJ, MD, VA, DC

WE ACCEPT INSURANCE

SERVICES

Home Health Aide

  • Our high-quality staff of home health aides provides personal care assistance. Visits can be for specific care needs, flexible hourly care, or dedicated full time.

  • Available to assist with actives of daily living such as: dressing, bathing, and grooming, meal preparation, medication reminders, and more.

On-call Nursing

  • Access to 24-hour nurse support

  • Nurse Practioner available for telehealth or in-person visits as needed

  • Social Worker available via phone on in person as needed

Medminders

  • Pill box refills

  • Coordination with a designated local pharmacy for medication refills, delivery, and med strip packaging for easy use (if desired)
