Hospice Care, is an interdisciplinary palliave care program delivered primarily in a home or skilled nursing seng, to individuals with life liming condions and who are no longer candidates or seeking aggressive treatments. Hospice provides a holisc approach to caring, by taking the paent and their loved ones under their wings, and providing, physical, spiritual and emoonal support under the skilled guidance of a Board-Cerfied Physician, a Nurse Case Manager, Clinical Social Worker, and Pastoral Support. Hospice provides durable medical equipment necessary for the safety and care of the paent, as well as medicaons for comfort and palliaon.
